Bill Taylor

Responsible Master Plumber · RMP 17356

Bill is a Responsible Master Plumber, licensed by the Texas State Board of Plumbing Examiners. He began his plumbing career working and training alongside his father, Olen "Corky" Ray, a Master Plumber who instilled in him the values of safety first, dedication, and responsibility — making sure every customer's needs are met with professionalism, safety, and a friendly attitude.

Bill has serviced the Greater Houston area's commercial plumbing repair market since 1985, working with everything from large national chains to independent businesses. He truly understands the needs and challenges of both the commercial and residential markets.

Deena Vlach Taylor

Operations & Customer Care

A Columbus, Texas resident since 1994, Deena has deep roots in this community and raised her three daughters here. She brings 25 years of experience in administrative assistance, customer service, contracts, logistics, and accounting to keep BOLD Plumbing running smoothly behind the scenes.

Deena makes sure every customer interaction reflects the same care and attention that's earned BOLD Plumbing its reputation across Colorado County.

A Legacy of
Craftsmanship

Bill's plumbing journey started alongside his father, Olen "Corky" Ray, a Master Plumber licensed in 1977. That same attitude of safety first and dedication to the customer has carried through every license, every job site, and every year since.

  • 1977

    Olen "Corky" Ray Earns Master Plumber License

    License M-9172, received June 23, 1977 — where Bill's training began.

  • 1985

    Journeyman License Earned

    Bill receives his Journeyman License and begins servicing the Greater Houston commercial plumbing market.

  • 1989

    Master Plumber License Earned

    Bill becomes a licensed Master Plumber, expanding his expertise across commercial and residential work.

  • 2002

    Responsible Master Plumber Endorsement

    Bill earns his RMP endorsement, the credential under which BOLD Plumbing operates today.
