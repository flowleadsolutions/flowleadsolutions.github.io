Our Story

Bangladesh's First
Amusement
Complex

Opened on 19 February 2002, Fantasy Kingdom is owned and operated by Concord Entertainment Co. Ltd — a sister concern of the prestigious Concord Group. Sprawling across nearly 20 acres in Ashulia, Savar, it stands as a pioneering landmark of entertainment in Bangladesh.

From heart-stopping roller coasters and wave-pool splashes to heritage discoveries and five-star resort retreats — every corner of Fantasy Kingdom Complex delivers the extraordinary.
